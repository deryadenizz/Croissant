How to Make Croissant

  • Step 1: Prepare the dough using flour, sugar, yeast, salt, butter, and milk.
  • Step 2: Chill the dough to enhance its texture.
  • Step 3: Roll and fold the dough multiple times to create layers.
  • Step 4: Re-chill the dough for optimal results.
  • Step 5: Roll out the dough to the desired thickness.
  • Step 6: Cut the dough into long, skinny triangles.
  • Step 7: Shape the triangles into a crescent form.
  • Step 8: Cover the croissants with plastic wrap and let them rise.
  • Step 9: Bake the croissants to golden perfection.
