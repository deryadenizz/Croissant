How to Make Croissant

  • 1- Make the dough with flour, sugar, yeast, salt, butter, and milk.
  • 2- Chill the dough.
  • 3- Roll and fold the dough 4-6 times.
  • 4- Re-Chill the dough.
  • 5-Roll out the dough to an appropriate thickness.
  • 6- Cut the dough into long skinny triangles.
  • 7-Roll the triangles into a crescent shape.
  • 8- Cover with plastic wrap and let rise.
  • 9- Bake.
